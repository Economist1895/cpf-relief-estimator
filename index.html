Voluntary CPF Contributions

ℹ️SEPs and platform workers may make voluntary contributions to their OA, SA, and MA. The tax relief allowed is the lowest of: (a) 37% of net trade income minus compulsory MediSave contributions; (b) the remaining CPF Annual Limit headroom after compulsory contributions; or (c) the actual amount contributed.
💡From 1 January 2022, employees cannot claim CPF Relief on voluntary contributions to their OA or SA. Voluntary MediSave top-ups made from that date are instead claimed under CPF Cash Top-up Relief — see the RSTU section below.
